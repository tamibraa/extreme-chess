HOW THE CHAOS WORKS
Atomic — every capture detonates: the capturing piece, the captured piece, and all non-pawn pieces on the 8 surrounding squares are vaporised. Blow up the enemy king to win instantly. Kings can't capture, and you can't make a blast that kills your own king.
King of the Hill — get your king onto a centre square (d4 d5 e4 e5) and you win on the spot.
Three-Check — check the enemy king three times total and you win, no mate needed.
Crazyhouse — captured enemy pieces join your bank. On your turn, tap a banked piece then an empty square to parachute it in.
Standard checkmate & stalemate still apply on top of all of it.
